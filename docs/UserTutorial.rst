Plant Tracer Web App Tutorial
=============================

Welcome and Registration
-------------------------
- Go to `app.planttracer.com`.
- Register for an account using your Course Key, which will have been previously provided by your Course Administrator.
- Enter your full name, email address, and Course Key.
- Look for an email from `admin@planttracer.com`. Click on the link to upload your first movie.

.. image:: webapp/docs/media/image1.png
   :alt: Plant Tracer Registration

Uploading Movies
----------------
- Enter the title of the file (choose an informative name) and a description of the movie.
- Choose a file to upload. Your movie file must be 64MB or less. If your file is larger than 64MB, you must use some other tool to reduce the file size.

.. image:: webapp/docs/media/image2.png
   :alt: Uploading Movies on Plant Tracer

Tracking Movie
--------------
- Once the file is uploaded, you can choose to track it.
- In the bottom left of the page, you will see "Track movie ‘Movie Title’ (#)".
  Click on that to track.
- You will need to move the 3 markers to the appropriate position in the first frame of your movie.
  Once they are in position, click the button "Track Movie".
- In the top left corner above the movie frame, you will see "Tracking movie…".
- The tracked movie will appear in place of the original. You can play and evaluate the position of the red dot as it tracks the apex of your plant.
  At this point, you can pause and note at which position there might be a loss of correct tracking.

.. image:: webapp/docs/media/image3.png
   :alt: Tracking Movie in Plant Tracer

Interpreting and Reading Results
--------------------------------
- You have the option of re-tracking the movie from that frame.
  Use the arrow buttons just below the original movie to navigate to the frame where tracking was lost.
  Then move the apex marker to the correct position. Now press the button "re-track movie".
- Graphs help visualize the horizontal (x position) and vertical (y position).
- When you are satisfied with the tracking, you can hit the button "Download Trackpoints".
- At this point, you are ready to use a spreadsheet to analyze and graph the data.
