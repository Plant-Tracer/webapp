Plant Motion Analysis

In demo mode, you may only play an already traced movie. You may also move markers, but the movement won't be saved. When logged in as a user (not in demo mode), you may add and delete markers, position them and have their new positions saved, and trace and re-trace movies. When in demo mode, these features are disabled.

To analyze the motion of the plant in the video:

  • Place the Apex marker on the plant's apex
  • Move the Ruler markers to the corresponding parts of the ruler in the video (if there is a ruler in the video)
  • Press the Trace movie or Re-trace movie button to initiate tracing

You may add additional markers to trace additional points of interest.

Once the movie is traced, you may play the traced movie using the controls on the movie player section of the page. You may play the whole movie from the current point, step forwards or backwards one frame at a time, or go to a specified frame.

If you notice that tracing a given marker has not been successful for the entire length of the movie, go find the frame where tracing is lost, re-position the marker, and press Re-trace movie to re-trace from that point forward. You may have to repeat this process.

Units and Conventions

Pixel coordinates are relative to the origin point (0,0) in the top left corner of the frame. Coordinate values increase from left to right and top to bottom.

In the Marker Table Location (mm) column, millimeter values are expressed as millimeters from the top left corner of the frame.

In the Time vs Position line graphs, the X Position and Y Position values are distances from the Apex marker's location in frame 0.

This version of PlantTracer does not calculate or display the time elapsed in the uploaded videos. Please refer to the Help for an example of how to calculate time in seconds from the frame values provided.
